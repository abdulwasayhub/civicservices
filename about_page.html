About Civic Services LLC

Our Story

Dedicated to Excellence in Logistics

With years of experience in the transportation and logistics industry, Civic Services LLC has built a reputation for reliability, integrity, and exceptional customer service. Our mission is to provide comprehensive truck dispatch services that help owner-operators and trucking companies maximize their profitability while minimizing their operational stress.

We understand the challenges of the trucking business, from finding profitable loads to managing paperwork and maintaining compliance. That's why we've assembled a team of experienced dispatchers and support staff who are committed to your success. Our 24/7 operation ensures that you're never alone on the road, and our extensive network of shippers and brokers means more opportunities for profitable hauls.
